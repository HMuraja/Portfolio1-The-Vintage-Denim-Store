About or Services

Fit and Style Consultation
Free of Cost

Are you after a little more than just a retail experience. We can help at finding a pair that you want to keep a little bit longer. If you book a consultation with our stylist, they can help you find the perfect pair. Our stylist will take your measurements and have chat about your style preferences, after this you will be given some pairs to try. This consultation is free of cost, but if you want to guarantee this service on busy days, we suggest you contact us to book an appointment.
